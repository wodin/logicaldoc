The LogicalDOC development team is happy to announce the immediate availability of LogicalDOC CE 7.2.1

You can get this distribution in tomcat-bundle archive from SourceForge: 
http://sourceforge.net/projects/logicaldoc/files/distribution/LogicalDOC%20CE%206.3/logicaldoc-7.2.1-tomcat-bundle.zip/download

LogicalDOC is a Web-based Document Management System (DMS) based on open 
source technology (GWT +Spring +Hibernate). Thanks to its intuitive interface, 
LogicalDOC transforms complex operations into easy tasks. It allows the 
storage, organization, versioning and use of all kind of files and documents 
within an user friendly interface.

Changes:
Improved support to preview documents
Fixed support for Locales with language variants (Portuguese-brazilian)
Completed the GUI translation in German language
Introduced support for the Norwegian language (Big thanks to Magnus Ottosen - Datawaves.no)
Improvements in the advanced search
Fixed problems on web-services
WebDAV Permission fixes on renaming, moving and deleting folder and file.
Improved parser for PDF documents
New Webservice method to collect the metadata of a list of documents


You can use our dedicated Bug tracker http://bugs.logicaldoc.com to report errors or feature requests.
