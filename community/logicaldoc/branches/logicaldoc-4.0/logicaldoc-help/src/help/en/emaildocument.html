Sending documents via email

The system offers the possibility to send documents via email.
Such functionality, essentially allows to generate a mail to a specific address, containing the selected document .

It will be sufficient to access to a folder with documents and from the Tools menu of the document (Right Click on the document), choose "Send as email".

To this point a mask will appear that will demand address email of the sender, the one of the adressee, the subject of the message and an optional text.

Note:  
It is recommended to specify the subject of the message and a brief descriptive text.
Some software anti-Spam can consider the email lacking in subject as Undesired and eliminate it automatically.
