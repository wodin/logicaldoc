Template

In LogicalDOC you can define some document types. Each of them has a name, a description, and one or more attributes. When you upload or edit a document, you can assign to it a template and specify the values of its attributes. In LogicalDOC EE there are two templates: the default template without attributes and the email template with three attributes: from to define the sender, to for the recipient and subject for the subject of the e-mail.
