Receiving system messages

In order to read own messages it is sufficient to access the main page of messages management: Personal data --> Messages.
It is also available a link present in the homepage of the system: "Messages".

First of all, in the available messages directory, appear the ones received showing the subject and the sender.
The received messages are visualized in a list sorted by reception date; those not still read are visualized in bold letter.
Notice the Priority (icon on the first column of the table) and the link for the fast erasure of the message.

Clicking on the elements of the directory, the content of the message will become visualized.
