LogicalDOC User's Guide

LogicalDOC is a system for archiving documents, it is easy to use and has been designed to be deployed into any corporate network. It is ideal for storing, sharing and searching electronic documents of any kind. This manual is dedicated to the staff responsible for the administration or use of document archiving.
